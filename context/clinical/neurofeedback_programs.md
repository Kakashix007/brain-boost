# Neurofeedback & Mental Techniques

Summary of the clinical programs and techniques used at NeuroMaroc.

## Key Documents Referenced

## Programs Overview
The clinic utilizes various neurofeedback protocols and mental techniques aimed at:
- **Peak Performance**: For executives and athletes.
- **Mental Health**: Support for anxiety, ADHD, and stress management.
- **Cognitive Enhancement**: Improving memory, focus, and brain health.
- **Brain Boost Pack**: A specific landing page product offering for intensive brain training.

## Techniques
- Quantitative EEG (qEEG) guided neurofeedback.
- Dynamic Neurofeedback.
- Complementary mental training techniques.
