# The INC Precision Pathway

A standardized clinical journey for patients.

## Key Documents Referenced
- `The_INC_Precision_Pathway.pdf`
- `The_INC_Longevity_Blueprint.pdf`

## The Pathway Steps
1. **Assessment**: Initial qEEG and clinical evaluation.
2. **Analysis**: Data-driven treatment planning.
3. **Training**: Intensive neurofeedback sessions.
4. **Integration**: Mental techniques and lifestyle adjustments.
5. **Evaluation**: Re-assessment and longevity planning.

## Focus on Longevity
The "Longevity Blueprint" integrates neurofeedback as a long-term strategy for brain health and anti-aging.
