# Clinical AI & Intelligence Strategy

This document outlines the integration of Artificial Intelligence into the clinical operations of NeuroMaroc.

## Key Documents Referenced
- `Clinical_AI_Blueprint.pdf`
- `Clinical_Intelligence_Strategy.pdf`
- `AI_Driven_Clinical_Growth.pdf`
- `Automating_Clinical_Excellence.pdf`

## Strategic Focus Areas
1. **Automated Clinical Excellence**: Using AI to monitor and enhance the quality of neurofeedback sessions.
2. **Clinical Intelligence**: Data mining clinical results to improve treatment protocols.
3. **AI Growth Bridge**: Connecting clinical data with marketing insights to target the right patients.
4. **Efficiency**: Reducing manual administrative tasks through AI-driven automation.

## Implementation Goals
- Real-time clinical feedback loops.
- AI-assisted diagnosis and treatment planning.
- Scalable clinical protocols that maintain high standards across the network.
