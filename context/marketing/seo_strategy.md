# SEO & Search Strategy

This document details the current SEO status and strategy for `neuromaroc.com`.

## Current Performance (Keywords)
Based on `top_keywords.csv`, the site ranks well for local intent:

| Keyword | Position | Search Volume | URL |
|---------|----------|---------------|-----|
| international neurofeedback center marrakech | 2 | 210 | neuromaroc.com/ |
| neurofeedback marrakech | 4 | 70 | neuromaroc.com/ |
| neurofeedback prix maroc | 17 | 140 | neuromaroc.com/ |
| international neurofeedback center casablanca photos | 27 | 260 | neuromaroc.com/nos-centres |
| neurofeedback agadir | 28 | 70 | neuromaroc.com/ |

### Strategic Insights
- **Strong Local Intent**: Dominating "Marrakech" related terms.
- **Opportunity**: Improve rankings for "Casablanca", "Agadir", and generic "Maroc" terms.
- **Content Gap**: Need more content around "prix" (pricing) and "photos" of centers to satisfy specific user queries.

## Backlink Profile
Analysis of `backlinks.csv` shows a mix of:
- **Aged Domain Redirects**: Using aged domains to boost authority.
- **SEO Services**: Several links from SEO agencies (`rankgrow.agency`, `seobacklinks.agency`).
- **Web Directories**: Listings in various web directories.

### Recommendations
- Diversify the link profile with more niche-relevant, clinical, and local Moroccan backlinks.
- Audit current backlinks to ensure they aren't triggering any "spam" signals (some look low quality).
