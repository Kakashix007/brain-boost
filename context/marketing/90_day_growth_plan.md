# 90-Day Digital Growth Plan

Summary of the short-term tactical plan for NeuroMaroc.

## Key Documents Referenced
- `Plan marketing digital sur 90 jours (franchise, client ciblé de qualité).docx`
- `Guide_Marketing_Digital_90Jours.pdf`
- `Plan_Marketing_90Jours_Simplifie.pdf`
- `30_Day_Growth_Engine.pdf`

## Phases of Growth
1. **Foundation (Days 1-30)**: Optimization of the current website, setting up tracking, and initial content push.
2. **Expansion (Days 31-60)**: Launching targeted ads, persona-based marketing, and localized SEO for Casablanca/Agadir.
3. **Scaling (Days 61-90)**: Refining the "Growth Engine", automation of lead nurturing, and franchise-focused marketing.

## Target Audience
- High-quality clients seeking neurofeedback.
- Potential franchise partners.
- Local residents in major Moroccan cities.

## Key Metrics (KPIs)
- Website traffic growth.
- Lead generation volume (specifically "quality" leads).
- Conversion rate optimization (CRO) on the landing pages.
