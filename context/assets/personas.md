# Marketing Personas

Target audiences defined for the growth strategy.

## Source
- `SLIDES PER PERSONA` (Directory)

## Key Personas (Likely)
1. **The High-Performer**: Focused on cognitive enhancement, productivity, and peak performance.
2. **The Stressed Professional**: Seeking relief from anxiety, burnout, and stress-related issues.
3. **The Concerned Parent**: Looking for support for children with ADHD, learning difficulties, or behavioral challenges.
4. **The Longevity Seeker**: Interested in brain health, anti-aging, and long-term wellness.
5. **The Potential Franchisee**: Business-minded individuals looking to invest in a clinical network.

## Content Strategy
Each persona has dedicated slide decks and messaging tailored to their specific pain points and desires.
